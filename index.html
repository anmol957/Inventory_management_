Welcome to InventoHome.
Keeping Every Corner Accounted For.

Simplifying your Home Inventory Management

User Friendly Platform

InventoHome provides a user-friendly experience, enabling effortless cataloging and management of household inventory. With a simple and intuitive interface, users can easily add, categorize, and update possessions without any hassle.

Insurance Claims

InventoHome also facilitates smoother insurance claims by providing comprehensive documentation of household items. In the event of loss or damage, the platform's detailed inventory records serve as valuable documentation, simplifying the claims process and ensuring a swift and accurate resolution.

Digital Access

InventoHome ensures secure and convenient digital access to all documentation, offering users the flexibility to manage their home inventory from anywhere. Through cloud-based storage, users can securely store and access their inventory data, eliminating the risk of physical document loss.
